Sustainability Commitments

Built for longevity, low waste, and material transparency.

We produce on demand, use FSC-certified substrates when available, and work with low-impact water-based pigments. Offcuts are reused in studio and sample production.

For project documentation, we can provide material details and care guidance on request.
